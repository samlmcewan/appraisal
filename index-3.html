Welcome to Medical Appraisal Scotland (home of SOAR)

Welcome to the Medical Appraisal Scotland website.

This web resource is designed to support doctors (in both Primary and Secondary Care) working and training in Scotland undertaking Appraisal and Revalidation.

The Scottish Online Appraisal Resource (SOAR) has a number of tools and resources to support both Appraisers and Appraisees in the appraisal process and also allows Trainees to complete their self declarations.
